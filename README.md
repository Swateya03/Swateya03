## About Me 🌏

2+ years across the full LLM lifecycle — **RLHF/SFT post-training and reward modeling** (research), **production RL environments & evaluation tooling** in Python (engineering), and **benchmark design, A/B testing & statistical error analysis** (data science) — for **Google, Meta, Amazon, Nvidia, OpenAI & Anthropic** engagements via Turing. IEEE-published researcher in reinforcement learning. I ship end-to-end: FastAPI, Docker, MLflow, CI/CD, and full LLM stacks with PyTorch, QLoRA, RAG, and LangGraph.

My independent research focuses on **reward hacking and AI alignment** — building empirical testbeds that measure whether agents actually solve tasks or merely game the signals that measure them. Key finding: reward hacking is ambiguity-driven, with hack rates rising from **0% to 80%** as task ambiguity increases.
